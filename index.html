CBD BUG’s Principles

The Brisbane CBD BUG is commited to bettering Brisbane’s transport network by making its streets safe to all road users. It’s guiding priciples are

  • Safety First – We support initiatives that prioritize cyclist safety, including protected bike lanes, lower speed limits, and better road designs.
  • Accessibility for All – We advocate for infrastructure and policies that make cycling a viable and inclusive option for people of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds.
  • Connected & Continuous Networks – We push for a well-planned cycling network that is safe, direct, and fully connected—not just isolated bike lanes that don’t link up.
  • Sustainability & Climate Action – We support policies that promote cycling as a low-emission, environmentally friendly mode of transport to help reduce Brisbane’s carbon footprint.
  • Collaboration & Community Engagement – We work with local government, businesses, and residents to ensure cycling improvements meet the needs of the community.
  • Evidence-Based Advocacy – Our decisions and campaigns are based on research, best practices, and real-world data to push for effective, proven solutions.
  • Equity & Fair Use of Public Space – Roads and infrastructure should be designed to support all road users, not just cars. We advocate for fair allocation of space to cycling, walking, and public transport.
  • Fun & Healthy Cities – Cycling should be enjoyable! We support projects that make Brisbane a more vibrant, active, and people-friendly city.

Why Should YOU Join?

Join our community to make a difference, connect with fellow cyclists, and help create a better riding experience for everyone!

  • Shape the Future of Cycling in Brisbane: By joining our advocacy group, you’ll have a direct say in the decisions that affect cycling infrastructure, policies, and programs in Brisbane.
  • Connect with Like-Minded Cyclists: Meet fellow cycling enthusiasts, share experiences, and learn from each other’s passion for cycling.
  • Stay Informed and Up-to-Date: Get the latest news, updates, and insights on cycling-related initiatives, events, and campaigns in Brisbane.
  • Amplify Your Voice: Join forces with other cyclists to advocate for safer roads, better infrastructure, and a more cycling-friendly city.
  • Contribute to a Healthier, More Sustainable Brisbane: Help create a city where cycling is a safe, convenient, and enjoyable transportation option, contributing to a healthier environment and community.
